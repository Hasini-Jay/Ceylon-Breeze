Our Story

Founded with purpose

In 2015, Priya Bandara imagined a Sri Lankan resort brand that would never trade conscience for comfort. Ceylon Breeze Resorts began as a single hillside retreat and grew into three distinct properties united by one promise: luxury that leaves the lightest footprint.

Our mission is eco-luxury travel that uplifts villages, protects habitat, and invites guests into honest cultural exchange. We source produce nearby, train young talent from surrounding communities, and design every stay to feel personal — never performative.

Our vision is to be Sri Lanka's most beloved independent resort brand — known for warmth, authenticity, and excellence. Our values anchor every decision: Sustainability, Authenticity, Warmth, and Excellence.

Our Green Pledge

Solar-Powered Facilities

We invest in rooftop solar arrays and efficient cooling systems so that comfort does not come at the cost of the climate guests come to admire.

Community Employment

More than eighty percent of our colleagues hail from neighbouring villages — bringing authentic stories and steady livelihoods home after every shift.

Zero Single-Use Plastics Policy

Reusable glass, refill stations, and compostable dining ware keep plastic away from beaches, trails, and kitchens across the chain.
