Our Three Retreats

Ceylon Breeze Sigiriya

Sigiriya, Central Province

Set where ancient jungle meets the legendary rock fortress, our Sigiriya retreat offers 24 rooms framed by birdsong and morning mist. Wander nature trails at dawn, cool off in the outdoor pool, and dine on seasonal island flavours as fireflies rise.

Pool ·Nature Trails ·Restaurant ·Spa ·Free WiFi

Ceylon Breeze Mirissa

Mirissa, Southern Province

Eighteen rooms open onto soft sand and rolling surf. Between November and April, blue whales glide past our horizon — best seen from our infinity pool or beach bar. Evenings end with tangerine skies and lantern-lit dinners.

Beach Access ·Whale Watching ·Beach Bar ·Spa ·Free WiFi

View Rooms

Ceylon Breeze Ella

Ella, Uva Province

Sixteen hillside rooms catch misty mornings above the Ella Gap. Cooler air invites slow hikes, tea estate walks, and fireside evenings. Our restaurant celebrates highland produce and Ceylon tea in every course.

Mountain Views ·Tea Walks ·Fireplace Rooms ·Restaurant ·Free WiFi
